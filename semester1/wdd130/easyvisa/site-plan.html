About Us

Easy Visa is a specialized consulting company for people who want to learn English in a short time within the United States. We have agreements with several institutions that teach English full-time in the United States and we will provide you with information and recommendations according to your needs. Easy visa will do all the paperwork for you, from the application to the school and the immigration paperwork, that is, until you have your student visa. Many students feel overwhelmed by the amount of paperwork that needs to be done in order to study in the United States, but not us, you won't feel that way anymore. Are you married or have a family and want to travel to the United States with your family to study a professional career but you do not know English? Easy Visa has the experience of applying to school and immigration procedures for you and your family. We are more than a company, we are your friends and we want you to succeed in this great goal. Before starting with the procedures, Easy Visa makes an evaluation of your profile and helps you make the right decisions so that you can go to study in the United States in the short term.
