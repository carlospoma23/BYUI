MAIN RIVERS

This page is to give information about the rivers that the company runs.

IDAHO

The Salmon is one of the longest undammed rivers in the United States. Let's not forget the rapids! They range from class I-IV. The Salmon is one of the longest undammed rivers in the United States.

UTAH

Desolation Canyon has been recognized as a National Landmark. This designation is based on the uniqueness and rich history that the canyon tells. Desolation is rated a Class II/III and is usually done in 6 days.

ARIZONA

Grand Canyon river rafting combines world-class whitewater with breathtaking scenery to make one truly unforgettable river experience. CLASS I - X: The Grand Canyon uses a unique river rating scale.
